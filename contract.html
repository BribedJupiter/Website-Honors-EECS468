Our Founder: John Smith

Our founder, John Smith, graduated top of his class from the College of Video Game Sciences at The University of America in 1996. After graduating, he went on to found Swordfight Studios and to personally develop one of the most loved video games of all time, Earthquake 1000. He earned his MBA from Harvard Business School in 2004, and, since stepping down as CEO in 2007, has taken on an executive producer role to spend more time with his family.
